What is RPA ?

Nowadays, despite the existence of many necessary applications used in business, still most of these solutions require more or less human intervention. As an example we can use CRM systems, which require employees who often perform repetitive, monotonous activities. Most of the structured processes can be fully or partially automated with the help of intelligent software robots. In the time saved, people can perform more creative tasks that will keep them satisfied with their work. This type of automation is called Robotic Process Automation - RPA for short. RPA implementation software should be simple and quick to use - that's what Robot Force is.
